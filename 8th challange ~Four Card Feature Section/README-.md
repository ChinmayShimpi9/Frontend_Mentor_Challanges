### The challenge

In these challange we are free to use flexbox or grid or all the other properties and the challange is simple to create the [Four Card Feature Section].
Which includes the cards which will display heading, paragraphs and its icon and to manage the cards in the way that two cards are in row and two are in columns.
And you have to use the flex or grid or other properties to align the cards in such way that it resembles to the design.
